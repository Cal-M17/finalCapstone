The Meal Planner.

Nothing makes eating healthier easier than knowing what you are going to eat in advance. Plan your meals for the week ahead with meals in your cookbook.

  • Plan for breakfast, second breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks or create your own!
  • Toggle macro and calorie information
  • Drag and drop meals into the planner
  • See your weekly eats at a glance

The Cookbook.

Create and store your own recipes or import from blogs around the net. When used altogether, the Cookbook will feed into the meal planner and help populate your shopping list.

  • Create or import your own recipes
  • Search the in built recipe selection for inspiration
  • Offline access to recipes
  • Cooking mode to keep your screen on and set timers

The Shopping List.

Keep track of what you need to prepare all your meals with a shopping list pulled from the ingredients in your chosen meals.

  • Organise your food into product groups
  • quick add for items you need to buy but aren't going to eat
  • Offline access to your list
  • Sort your list by where you are going to buy your groceries
